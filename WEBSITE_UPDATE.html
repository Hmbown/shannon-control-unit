A Different Approach

While everyone's focused on model architectures and data, we asked a different question: Why are we manually tuning AI when autopilots have existed since 1912?

The Shannon Control Unit applies aerospace control theory to neural network training. Early results suggest this could eliminate hyperparameter search entirely. We're sharing our research as we validate on larger models.
